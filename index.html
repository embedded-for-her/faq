  • Instructors

  • Who will be the instructors?

    The instructors will be people who develop embedded systems for a living. They have through the entire life cycle of developing a product as a part of a crowdfunding campaign. And having been participants and speakers in multiple training programs, they have good experience in teaching an audience with a varied knowledge base.

  • So the participants have to interact with only a handful of instructors for 10 weeks?

    There will be guest speakers providing their unique insight right from freelancing, working with the maker community, being an author of technical books, starting a company and making stuff in general.

  • Fee

  • What can I do if I can't afford the 5000 Rs as the fee for the program?

    Please let us know in the application form and motivate the reason for your participation in this program. We'll see what we can do to subsidise the cost for your participation.

  • Privacy

  • What do you plan to do with the information that I submit in the application form?

    We only use the information received in the application form to select the candidates for the program. We will not share this information with any external entities.

  • Attendance

  • I will not be able to attend on a particular date. Would this be a problem?

    Please do try to attend all the class, especially the first five classes where the primary learning happens. Missing a session during the project could work, depending on the coordination with your teammates.
